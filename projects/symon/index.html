Symon

Symon is a contemporary townhome community characterized by minimalism and simplicity, offering affordable and functional homes. Located in the vibrant neighborhood of Evanston, Symon features attractive green belts and is designed to connect neighbors and inspire active living. With amenities like nearby schools, playgrounds, and essential services, Symon provides a welcoming and practical living environment for families and individuals alike.
